How to Make Es Kepal Milo

Ingredients

  • 3 sachets of Milo (± 60 grams)
  • 4 sachets of sweetened condensed milk (± 200 grams)
  • water (optional, to thin the sauce)
  • Ice cubes, as needed
  • 1 sachet of Milo powder for topping
  • 2 pieces of Oreo biscuits (coarsely crushed)

Tools

  • Medium-sized bowl
  • Tablespoon
  • Glass or cup
  • Ice crusher machine
  • Serving bowl

Steps to Make

  1. First, prepare a clean bowl. Add 3 sachets of Milo and 4 sachets of sweetened condensed milk to the bowl.
  2. Then, stir both ingredients with a spoon until well combined and forms a thick sauce.
  3. If the sauce is too thick and hard to pour, add 1–2 tablespoons water little by little while stirring until the Milo sauce becomes thinner and easier to pour.
  4. Next, prepare the ice cubes. Crush the ice using the machine until it resembles shaved ice. If you don't have a blender, place the ice in a plastic bag and crush it using a mortar or something hard.
  5. Then, place the serving bowl under the shaved ice machine. Shape the ice into a compact round ball using a spoon or plastic-wrapped hands.
  6. After that, pour the Milo sauce evenly over the shaved ice.
  7. Next, sprinkle Milo powder from 1 sachet over the sauce as an additional chocolate flavor layer.
  8. Then, add the crushed Oreo biscuit crumbs over the Milo powder as the main topping. You can adjust the texture of the crumbs, either fine or coarse, according to your preference.
  9. Finally, serve your Es Kepal Milo cold for the best experience.
